Casting Sandbox
Build a shoot's cast here by dragging people into priority order. Nothing is saved to Monday until you choose to — follow the steps below.
1
Pick a shoot tag
Loads everyone tagged for this shoot.
2
Arrange the cast
Drag each person into the BOTTOM, VERS, or TOP column — or into that column's ALTERNATES row. Order matters: the top slot is first priority. On a touchscreen, tap a person, then tap the slot you want them in. Use the ▾ button on each card to log their pay & dates (grey = nothing yet, yellow = half done, green = all set).
3
Save or share your work (optional)
“Save” stores this arrangement on Monday so any coworker can open it later. “Share link” copies a link that opens this exact layout. Neither changes the casting board.

Send the cast to a casting row
When you're happy with the order, pick the casting row this shoot belongs to and send it over. You'll see a preview before anything is written. This is the only step that changes the casting board.
